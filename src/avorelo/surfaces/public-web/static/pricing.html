There is nothing to buy

Avorelo Community Edition is local-first software that runs on your own machine. There are no paid plans, no accounts, and no hosted service to subscribe to.

Current status

The previous hosted paid service has been discontinued. There are no paid plans, accounts, subscriptions, checkout, or customer portal. Nothing is billed.

Avorelo is Open Source under the Apache License 2.0. The software license is $0. Personal, internal, organizational, and commercial use are all permitted — use, modify, and redistribute under Apache-2.0. See the license page.

The Apache-2.0 software is provided "as is" with no warranty and no included support. Optional paid services (support, implementation, consulting, integrations, training, hosted operation, maintenance, SLA) may be agreed separately and are never required to use Avorelo.

Services inquiries go to support@avorelo.com. Contacting that address does not change your Apache-2.0 rights.

Local-first

Avorelo runs as a command-line tool on your machine. The work it does, and the evidence it produces, stay on the machine that produced them.

  • ✓Receipts and evidence are written to local files
  • ✓The local viewer reads those files directly
  • ✓No cloud storage of your work

No account or hosted cloud

There is no sign-up, no login, and no activation server. Avorelo does not need an identity to work, because there is no service on the other end.

  • ✓No account, password, or session
  • ✓No hosted API and no cloud sync
  • ✓No automatic telemetry

Open Source (Apache-2.0)

Avorelo is Open Source, not a product with tiers. Apache-2.0 grants everyone the right to use, study, modify, and redistribute it — personally, internally, organizationally, and commercially — at no cost.

Common questions

Is there a paid plan?

No. The hosted paid service was discontinued. There are no plans, no tiers, and no purchase of any kind on this site.

I had a subscription. What happened to it?

The hosted service and its billing were discontinued. There are no active subscriptions and nothing renews. See the Terms for the website terms.

Do I need an account to use Avorelo?

No. Avorelo runs locally through its command-line interface. There is no sign-up and no activation server to contact.

Is my data safe?

Avorelo is local-first. Workflow data, proof, and evidence stay on your machine. It does not store raw prompts or raw code, and it does not send them anywhere. The Privacy page describes exactly what does and does not leave your machine.

When will the source be public?

Licensing and public availability are still being prepared. Until that is done, this site will not link to a repository or a published package, because neither is available yet.
