Launch Readiness

Launch Readiness is currently a coordination and truth layer. Avorelo can surface the blockers and the supporting signals, but it does not currently ship a dedicated launch execution engine that can complete the launch on your behalf.

Current truth

ROUTING_ONLY

Primary evidence

Launch review docs, production-confidence signals, and review helpers.

Runtime surface

Readiness and blocker reporting only.

Tests

Indirectly covered by readiness, public-surface, and release-boundary suites.

What exists today

Avorelo can keep launch blockers explicit across packaging, proof, and public-copy truth so a founder or reviewer is not working from guesswork.

Claim boundary

Launch Readiness does not onboard customers, does not send launch emails, and does not perform a production deploy. Those are explicit human actions outside the current canonical execution boundary.

What it does not do

It does not imply public launch is already approved, and it does not remove the need for pricing, legal, billing, or founder decisions before launch.
