Legal & Compliance Review Support

This capability page describes a bounded support lane: Avorelo can help identify protected concerns and stop unsafe automation, but it does not currently ship a dedicated legal-compliance execution engine in 0.3.0.

Current truth

ROUTING_ONLY

Primary evidence

Protected concern escalation boundaries, release hard stops, and legal source docs.

Runtime surface

Escalation, blocking, and documentation support only.

Tests

Covered indirectly by safety gates and release-boundary tests rather than a dedicated legal capability runner.

What exists today

Avorelo can classify risky work as needing approval, keep customer, billing, launch, and domain actions gated, and keep legal source documents explicit about draft status and human review requirements.

Claim boundary

Avorelo does not provide legal advice, does not certify compliance, and does not replace legal counsel. Legal and compliance review support means bounded escalation support, not automated legal verdicts.

What it does not do

It does not issue compliance guarantees, and it does not authorize billing, publish, or launch steps that require counsel or founder approval.
