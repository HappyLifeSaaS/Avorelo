There is no hosted dashboard

Avorelo is a local-first, Open Source CLI under Apache-2.0. It has no account, no hosted backend, and no hosted dashboard. Receipts and proof of what changed are written locally under .avorelo/ and viewed on your own machine with the local Control Center:

avorelo control-center --target .  ·  avorelo open
