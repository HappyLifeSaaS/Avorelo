# Support

Avorelo is a local-first CLI. It runs entirely on your machine, keeps your data local, and uploads
nothing automatically.

## Getting help

- **General support and questions:** [support@avorelo.com](mailto:support@avorelo.com?subject=Avorelo%20support)
- **Bugs and defects:** open a public issue at
  <https://github.com/HappyLifeSaaS/Avorelo/issues>
- **Optional paid services:** see [COMMERCIAL-SERVICES.md](COMMERCIAL-SERVICES.md)
- **Security vulnerabilities:** see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) — do **not** use a public issue

## Support bundles are local

`avorelo support bundle` writes a sanitized, allowlisted JSON + Markdown artifact under
`.avorelo/support/` in your project. It is created **on your machine** and **nothing is uploaded**.
Secrets, source, environment values, prompts, logs, and diffs are excluded by construction.

Review the files yourself first, then attach them to a GitHub issue or an email **only if you choose
to**. Do not include secrets, credentials, or private source in anything you send.

## Service level

Avorelo is Open Source under Apache-2.0, which is provided "as is" with no warranty and no included
support. Community support is **best-effort**. A guaranteed response time or SLA applies only under a
separate written services agreement — see [COMMERCIAL-SERVICES.md](COMMERCIAL-SERVICES.md).
